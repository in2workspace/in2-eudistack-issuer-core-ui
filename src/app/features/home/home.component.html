This site is intended for Legal Representatives of companies who want to issue one or more LEARCredentials to one or more employees of the company. A LEARCredential is a type of Verifiable Credential which enables an employee, nominated by a legal representative, to act on behalf of an organisation with restricted power with respect to third-parties.

  • The issuer of the LEARCredential must be a legal representative of the company. The legal representative will sign the LEARCredential with an eIDAS digital certificate, which can be either a personal one or a certificate of representation.
  • The receiver of the LEARCredential (both the subject and holder of the credential) can be any employee (or contractor) of the company. The legal representative will delegate a restricted set of power to that person. Those restricted power are included inside the credential and can be verified by any Relying party to whom the holder presents the LEARCredential.

You must be a legal representative of the company, to be able to delegate the restricted power required for DOME.

You need an eIDAS certificate to be able to use the system. In addition you will have to register your company email.

Note: if you are reading this in your mobile, most probably the button will not work, unless you have installed your eIDAS certificate in the mobile. You typically need to use a PC, with the certificate installed in the browser.

Obtain your wallet

You need a wallet to store your LEARCredentials and present them to Relaying parties like DOME Marketplace.

DOME provides a simple wallet enough for authenticating to the DOME Marketplace and performing all needed processes.

Scan this QR code or go in your mobile to wallet.dome-marketplace.org

Test the credential with a Verifier

Use your LEARCredential in your wallet to authenticate to a test Verifier, without user or password.

In this way you can see how everything works and will become familiar with the flows.
